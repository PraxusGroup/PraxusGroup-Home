Praxus is an international, multi-gaming, clan and guild in all aspects of the definition.

Praxus Group can be regarded as the umbrella guild under which everyone is a member regardless of what game they play with us.
This umbrella guild is referred to as "The Community".

Our mission is the Community

The Praxus mission has been defined from the outset as one that fosters a community of gaming enthusiasts. One that enables it's members to form friendships and develop each others skills. We do this by encouraging community solutions and organization, and by providing the tools our members need to get more out of their gaming experiences.

Is Praxus for you?

Praxus is what YOU make it

We don't dictate your gaming experience, we’re not your mom, we’re not your overlords. Solutions must come from within. That’s what makes a community great, all of us pulling together. If there's something we are missing our members are expected to craft or participate in the solutions we need.

You MUST be 18 years of age, or older
to join Praxus Group

New members complete a 30 day "probationary"
status before becoming full members

Do We sound like Your people?

  1. Create an account on our Community Forums
  2. Create a new topic in the Applicants section of the forums, with your name in the title and inform us of your intention to join.
  3. Join The official Discord channel.
  4. Contact a Community Admin on Discord to complete your joining process.
  5. Say hi, have fun, be Praxus.
